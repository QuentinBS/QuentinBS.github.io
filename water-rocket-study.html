Study purpose

The project is intended to examine how design and launch conditions affect a simple rocket system in flight. It should create a clear record of changes, observations, and measurable outcomes.

  • Connect classroom physics to a visible and repeatable launch system.
  • Compare how variables influence height, stability, and distance.
  • Develop stronger project documentation habits through repeated testing.

Planned evidence

This page is ready to hold multiple forms of documentation as the project grows.

  • Photos of the rocket body, fin setup, and launch stand.
  • Videos or still frames from each launch trial.
  • Tables of conditions and results for each run.
  • Short notes on what changed and what was observed.
